# Scope

## MVP Features

- **Business Profiles:** Capture quick descriptions for kiosks, boda riders, mitumba sellers, pastry makers, and small-scale farmers.
- **AI Advice:** Provide pricing, branding, competition, and marketing guidance tailored to the selected business type.
- **Business Plan Generator:** Produce a one-page plan with overview, steps, risks, marketing, and finances sections.
- **Financial Literacy Tips:** Deliver curated savings, chama, and inventory tips for daily resilience.
- **Cashflow Tracker:** Let users add income/expense entries, view daily/weekly/monthly rollups, and format all values in Kenyan shillings.

## V1.1 Backlog

- Export generated business plan to PDF for printouts.
- Import/export CSV for cashflow data to aid external accounting apps.
- Optional M-Pesa STK push logging (record references only; no live integration in MVP).
